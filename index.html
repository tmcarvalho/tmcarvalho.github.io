Biography

I received the master’s degree in 2019 from the Faculty of Sciences, University of Porto in Network and Information Systems Engineering. I am currently working toward a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at FCUP. My thesis focus on “Automated Privacy-Preserving Strategies”.

I am an enthusiast for machine learning systems that help in healthcare, environmental issues, and business growth. However, the data for such systems often contain personal information. My main research focuses on these two main topics: machine learning and data privacy. In more detail, my research interests focus on the de-identification process to ensure data privacy, including disclosure risk and utility through predictive performance, where AutoML aims to speed up the learning process.
