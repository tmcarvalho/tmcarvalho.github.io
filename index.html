Biography

I received my Master’s degree in Network and Information Systems Engineering from the Faculty of Sciences, University of Porto, in 2019. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at FCUP, with a dedicated research focus on “Automated Privacy-Preserving Strategies”.

In the context of machine learning and privacy, I have a particular interest in promoting the responsible and ethical use of data-driven technologies. My research focuses on secure data sharing using machine learning. Specifically, I’ve been studying meta-learning which plays an important role in finding optimal privacy-preserving solutions.
