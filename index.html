Biography

I received the master’s degree in 2019 from the Faculty of Sciences, University of Porto in Network and Information Systems Engineering. I am currently working toward a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at FCUP. My thesis focus on “Automated Privacy-Preserving Strategies”. My main research interests are dedicated to data privacy-preservation techniques, including the risk of identity disclosure and data utility through the efficiency and accuracy of machine learning. My experiments focus on the analysis of the effectiveness of such techniques in re-identification risk as well the consequent impact on predictive performance.
